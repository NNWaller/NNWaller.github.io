My Story

Carl Bard said, “Though no one can go back and make a brand new start, anyone can start from now and make a brand new ending.” These words eloquently sum up how I feel about pursuing a career as a software engineer/web developer.

I have always enjoyed solving problems and making things easier for others. The majority of my employment experience has been in the field of customer service. I have earned a bachelors degree in Family and Consumer Sciences from The University of Georgia. I have also completed a Masters Degree in Business Administration from Augsburg College and am a member of Women Who Code Atlanta.

I have become very proficient in Ruby. I also have some experience with HTML, CSS, the AMP web component framework, and JavaScript. I am currently learning more about Ruby on Rails, MongoDB, Blockchain Technology, and testing with RSpec and Capybara.
