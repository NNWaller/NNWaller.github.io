My Story

Carl Bard said, “Though no one can go back and make a brand new start, anyone can start from now and make a brand new ending.” These words eloquently sum up how I feel about pursuing a career as a software engineer.

I have always enjoyed solving problems and making things easier for others. The majority of my employment experience has been in customer service within the hospitality, insurance, and banking industries. I have earned a bachelors degree in Family and Consumer Sciences from The University of Georgia and completed a Masters Degree in Business Administration from Augsburg College. For the past year, I have been teaching myself to become a software engineer by using various online resources.

My favorite programming language is Ruby. I also have some experience with JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I am strengthening my skills in Rails, RSpec, Capybara, SQL, and MongoDB. I also have plans to learn GoLang and Angular in the near future. Blockchain and AI technologies have captured my interest, so I try and stay informed on those topics as well. I am a member of Women Who Code Atlanta.
