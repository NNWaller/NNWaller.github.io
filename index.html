My Story

Carl Bard said, “Though no one can go back and make a brand new start, anyone can start from now and make a brand new ending.” These words eloquently sum up how I feel about pursuing a career as a software engineer.

I have always enjoyed solving problems and making things easier for others. The majority of my employment experience has been in customer service within the hospitality, insurance, and banking industries. I have earned a bachelors degree in Family and Consumer Sciences from The University of Georgia and completed a Masters Degree in Business Administration from Augsburg College. For the past year, I have been teaching myself to code through various online resources.

My favorite programming language is Ruby. I also have some experience with JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I am strengthening my skills in Rails, MongoDB, and Test Driven Development. I have a growing interest in Blockchain technology and GoLang. I am also wide open to employment opportunities.
